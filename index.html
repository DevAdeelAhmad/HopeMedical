Companion services

Companion services are home-based supervision and monitoring activities which assist and/or instruct an individual in maintaining a comfortable and supportive environment when he or she is unable to do so alone. Companion Care Services. will not provide hands-on care for the client (such as feeding and dressing) but will provide companionship, provide respite for the main caregiver, offer encouragement and perform light housekeeping tasks. Duties may include (Social events and friend visits Shopping and errands, Personal correspondence and memoirs, Prompts and reminders ,Scrap booking, Gardening, Home projects

Overnight Care

This services involves a caregiver assisting with a few hours of care before bed and after a good night’s rest. If you are assisting a loved one, you know that your health can be affected without a good night’s rest. Caregivers usually request that they receive at least 8 hours of sleep, but they are there to assist you during the night if your loved one is unable to sleep or needs to use the restroom. Having a caregiver in the home as your loved one sleeps gives peace of mind and security while you rest, enabling you to wake up fresh to go about your day.
